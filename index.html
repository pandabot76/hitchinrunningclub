Who we are

A club built around the run, not the result

Hitchin Running Club (the Hitchin Hares) is a friendly bunch who like to meet up socially for some running — some of us take it more seriously than others. It's a great way to meet like-minded people, have some fun, and get fit in the process.

No trials, no minimum pace, no judgement. Sign up via Facebook to say hello before your first session.
